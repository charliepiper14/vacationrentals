Demand Gen YouTube Build - Detailed Planning Ahead of June 15 Launch

Campaign Architecture Ready, Sequenced to Land the Day Creative Arrives

Production team is contracted, filming next week (weather permitting), edit cycle through to June 15 delivery. The campaign build sits behind the creative on the critical path. The structure detailed below is the working blueprint, designed around the 90-day capture window from mid-June through mid-September that feeds the remarketing pool for the January through July 2026 booking season. Three campaigns (one per market), nine concept variants total (three per market), three creative durations (30s, 15s, 6s), three aspect ratios per cut. The audience capture mechanism is the central purpose: building the warm pool that the next twelve months of marketing activity will draw down against.

The Strategic Purpose

Why Demand Gen, Why Now

Demand Gen runs across YouTube in-stream, Shorts, in-feed, and Discover. It is the right Google product for the audience capture purpose. The targeting model serves the message to people who are in-market for vacation rentals in the LBI, Wildwood, and OCNJ geographies during the high-intent summer window. The conversion focus is not the immediate click. The conversion focus is the view event itself, which builds an audience signal that can be remarketed to in the dormant winter months and reactivated for the December 26 booking surge through Q1 of 2027.

The Twelve Month Audience Flywheel

Audience captured June through September feeds:

  • January through March 2027: early booking window remarketing, the strongest sustained intent period for the portfolio after summer itself
  • April through May 2027: shoulder week remarketing as season approaches
  • June through September 2027: in-summer return audience for the same vacationers
  • October through December 2027: November and December retargeting toward the December 26 surge
