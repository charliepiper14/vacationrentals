Core Strategic Pillars

1. Generic Search First

  • Generic outperforms PMax across all three accounts. This is the Q1 scaling foundation.
  • VRLBI Generic: $0.92 CPA | VRWW Generic: $4.65 CPA | VROCNJ Generic: $3.77 CPA
  • Prioritize Generic budget increases over PMax expansion
  • Use PMax for reach and brand awareness, Generic for conversion volume

2. Device Optimization by Account

  • VRLBI: Maintain balanced bids. Both devices performing well.
  • VRWW: Flip required. Increase desktop 20%, reduce mobile 20%.
  • VROCNJ: Maintain current structure. Mobile slightly winning.
  • Daily device monitoring through January to validate optimizations

3. Capture 2026 Booking Demand

  • 2026 queries already showing strong volume across all accounts
  • Add 2026-specific ad copy and extensions to all campaigns
  • Front-load budget in January through April per Duane's guidance to book guests early
  • Monitor booking patterns from lead analysis data to optimize timing

4. Efficiency Guardrails

  • 15% Rule: If 25% budget increase causes more than 15% CPA increase, pause and optimize
  • Daily monitoring: Check all accounts daily during January scaling phase
  • Quick response: Implement bid adjustments within 48 hours of trend detection
  • Reallocation flexibility: Move budget from underperforming to outperforming accounts
