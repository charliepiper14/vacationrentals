The Core Issue: CVR Pullback Across the Portfolio

Conversion Rate Declined on Every Campaign This Period

Spend was flat, clicks were up slightly, but conversions fell sharply because CVR dropped on all five active campaigns. VRLBI Generic fell from 25.13% to 20.28%. VRLBI PMax from 26.80% to 14.92%. VRWW Generic from 11.09% to 9.65%. VROCNJ PMax from 12.45% to 6.81%. VROCNJ Generic from 12.02% to 7.53%. This is a portfolio-wide conversion rate event, not an isolated campaign problem. The most likely causes are seasonal demand quality shift (mid-April vs late March search intent), landing page performance under higher traffic loads, and in the case of VROCNJ, a measurable quality score deterioration confirmed by rank lost IS data.

What Improved vs Prior Period
