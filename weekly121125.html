Performance Decline Analysis & Optimization Strategy

Evidence of Quality Score/Ranking Issues:
  • Lost IS (Rank) at 77.58%: Increased from 71.26% last week (+8.9%), indicating we're losing auctions due to ad rank, not budget. This is the primary driver of conversion decline.
  • Search Impression Share Growing But Inefficiently: Improved to 11.60% from <10%, but winning lower-quality auction positions during off-peak times
  • Conversion Rate Collapse: Dropped from 29.38% to 12.16% (-58.6%) indicates landing page experience or ad relevance degradation
  • Off-Season Amplification: November typically sees 40-50% volume drops before Dec 26 surge, but quality issues are amplifying the natural decline
  • Both Campaigns Affected: PMax (+59.5% CPA) and Generic (+188% CPA) both declining suggests account-wide quality score work needed
Immediate Optimization Plan:
  • Ad Relevance Refresh: Update RSAs with 2026 season messaging, test new headline/description combinations focused on "early booking" value props
  • Landing Page Experience Audit: Review page speed, mobile experience, form friction points. Mobile converts at different rate than desktop.
  • Expected CTR Improvements: Add more specific location modifiers to ads, implement countdown timers for Dec 26 surge
  • Negative Keyword Expansion: Audit search terms for irrelevant traffic consuming budget at low conversion rates
  • Bid Strategy Adjustment: Consider shifting to Maximize Conversion Value or Target ROAS to prioritize quality over volume during off-season
